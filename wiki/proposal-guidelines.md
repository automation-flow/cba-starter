# Proposal guidelines

Structure and voice for every proposal. The [[guy]] agent follows this.

> Example Brain content for the starter. Adjust to your own structure and voice.

## Structure
1. **The problem** — in the client's own words, pulled from the call.
2. **What we'll build** — the solution, plainly. No jargon.
3. **How it works** — the Capture, Brain, Act shape: what we record, the
   knowledge it becomes, the agent that acts.
4. **Scope** — what's in, and what's explicitly out.
5. **Timeline** — phases, not a single date.
6. **Price** — one number, or two options, from [[pricing]].
7. **Next step** — one clear action.

## Voice
- Write like a person, not a brochure. Short sentences. Say what it does.
- Drop "leverage", "seamless", "cutting-edge", and the like. No em dashes.
- Lead with the client's outcome, not our technology.
- If the call was in Hebrew, write the proposal in Hebrew.
